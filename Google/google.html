I am currently working on a 20% project with the GCP/TI Education Team (GET) on their pilot training module called Art of Reliability (ART).

The ART program is a new initiative focused on increasing the adoption of AI tools among Site Reliability Engineers (SREs) to enhance reliability practices.

This is a strategic multi-year project with an initial focus on AI-Driven Operations, aiming to transform how reliability is maintained at scale.
