ART of Reliability Training Hub

Role UX Designer
Team Rachel Wu, Jenny Fandrianto, Laura Unal, Preston Yoshioka
Duration 2025-Present

Mission

Transform how reliability is maintained at scale by increasing the adoption of AI tools among Site Reliability Engineers (SREs) through a strategic, multi-year training initiative.

My Work & Impact

During the ART pilot, I designed a high-engagement learning experience that activated 2,559 unique users and achieved a 28.8% completion rate. By optimizing the landing page and core training flows, I drove 4,809 sessions with a strong average engagement time of 21:07 minutes, ultimately exceeding the AI study participation goal by 64%. These results validated the platform’s scalability and successfully converted user interest into measurable educational growth.
